To setup your Salesforce app in Cobalt for OAuth, you will need the following credentials from your [Salesforce developer account](https://developer.salesforce.com):
- Consumer Key
- Consumer Secret

If you haven't already created an app in Salesforce, you'd need to create one.

### Creating an app in Salesforce

To create a Salesforce app and aquire the above mentioned credentials, please follow the steps mentioned below:

1. Log in to your Salesforce dashboard with your developer account credentials.
2. Once you are in, find the `Setup` button - Gear icon on the top right of the dashboard.
3. Click on the Setup button and select `Apps` > `App Manager` options from the side menu bar under `Platform Tools` section.
4. Select `New Connected App` in the setup page.
5. Enter the basic details of your application
6. Enable OAuth settings
7. Enter the Callback URL as provided in your Salesforce settings page in Cobalt. Go to your [apps catalog](https://app.gocobalt.io/apps) > Salesforce > Settings > Use your credentials.
