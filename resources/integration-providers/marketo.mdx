<Note>
Marketo is an **API Key** based integration and does not require the setup of an app.
</Note>

### Required Credentials of a Linked Account

To successfully connect with the **Marketo** integration, a Linked Account or an end-user will need to provide the following to connect:

1. Client ID
2. Client Secret
3. REST Endpoint
4. Identity Endpoint

<Info>
To understand how a Linked Account can get the above mentioned credentials, refer [here](https://docs.gocobalt.io/resources/integration-providers/marketo#getting-credentials-of-marketo).
</Info>


### Getting Credentials of Marketo

To acquire the required credentials and connect a Linked Account, please follow the steps mentioned below:
<img height="200" src="/images/Marketo/marketo_config.png" alt="Connect a Linked Account"/>

1. Log in to your Marketo account.
2. Click on the `Admin` option in the top nav bar and select `LaunchPoint` under `Integration` in the side menu .
<img height="200" src="/images/Marketo/marketo_navigation.png" alt="Navigation in Marketo"/>
3. Click on `New` > `New Service` option. 
4. Enter the `Display Name` for your application, select `Service` as **Custom**, give it a description, select the user in `API Only User` and click on `Create`.
5. Select the **Custom_app** created and click on `View Details`. Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** of the user.
<img height="200" src="/images/Marketo/marketo_credentials.png" alt="Getting Credentials"/>

6. Click on `Web Services` under **Integration** in the side menu > Scroll down to `REST API` section and copy the **REST Endpoint** and **Identity**.
<img height="200" src="/images/Marketo/marketo_endpoint.png" alt="Getting Endpoints"/>

The Linked Account or end-user now have all the credentials required to connect with Marketo.
